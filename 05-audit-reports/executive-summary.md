# Executive Summary - BankSecure IT Audit

## Overview

This audit assessed BankSecure's IT environment against ISO 27001 and NIST CSF to identify risks, control gaps, and improvement areas.

## Key Findings

* High risk of unauthorized system access
* Weak change management
* No disaster recovery testing
* Limited monitoring

## Risk Exposure

|**LEVEL**|**COUNT**|
|-|-|
|High|2|
|Medium|2|
|Low|0|

## Recommendations

* Enforce MFA and RBAC
* Implement formal change approval
* Test DR annually
* Deploy centralized logging

## Conclusion

Improving governance and monitoring will significantly reduce operational and regulatory risk.
