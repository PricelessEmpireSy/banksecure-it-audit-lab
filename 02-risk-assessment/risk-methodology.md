# IT Risk Assessment Methodology – BankSecure

## Frameworks Referenced
- NIST SP 800-30 (Risk Assessment)
- ISO/IEC 27005 (Information Security Risk Management)

## Scoring Model
| Impact | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Low | 1 |
| Medium | 2 |
| High | 3 |

| Likelihood | Score |
|------------|-------|
| Low | 1 |
| Medium | 2 |
| High | 3 |

Risk Level = Impact × Likelihood

## Risk Rating
- 1–2 = Low  
- 3–4 = Medium  
- 6–9 = High
